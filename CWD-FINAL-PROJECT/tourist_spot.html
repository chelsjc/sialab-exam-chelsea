Start your journey to El Nido, Palawan today and experience the magic of this tropical paradise.

Discover Paradise in the Philippines

Nestled in the westernmost part of the Philippines, El Nido is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. With its mesmerizing beauty, crystal-clear waters, and captivating landscapes, it's no wonder that El Nido is hailed as one of the most enchanting destinations in the world.

A Natural Wonderland

El Nido boasts an exquisite natural backdrop that includes limestone karst formations that rise dramatically from the sea. These unique formations have been meticulously shaped by the hands of time and nature, making them a must-see wonder.

Discover the Chocolate Hills in Bohol

A Geological Marvel

The Chocolate Hills consist of an estimated 1,268 perfectly cone-shaped hills that stretch as far as the eye can see. These peculiar hills, which vary in size and shape, are made of limestone, coral, and marine deposits, formed millions of years ago. Their unique and uniform appearance has led to countless legends and stories about their origins.

The Mysterious Transformation

While these hills remain lush and green during the rainy season, it's during the dry months that they unveil their breathtaking transformation. As the grass covering the hills dries up and takes on a chocolate-brown hue, they appear like a sea of chocolate kisses, a sight that has earned them their sweet name.

Adventure and Exploration

Exploring the Chocolate Hills is an adventure that takes you through rural Bohol. You can hike to the summit of some of the hills and take in the landscape from different perspectives. The surrounding areas are also filled with lush greenery, serene villages, and picturesque scenes waiting to be discovered.

Enchanted River: Hidden in the heart of Surigao del Sur

The River's Mystery

The Enchanted River is shrouded in mystery, beginning with its impossibly clear, sapphire-blue waters. Fed by underwater springs, this river seems to glow as if touched by some ancient magic. The water is so translucent that you can see the riverbed, which adds to the illusion of enchantment.

The Legend and Lore

Local legends and stories surround the river, offering explanations for its uncanny beauty and its name. One tale speaks of fairies visiting the river at night, causing its bewitching glow. Another legend warns of a spellbinding sea nymph guarding its depths, preventing anyone from taking a dip in her precious waters.

The Daily Ritual

The Enchanted River is not just a sight to behold; it's also a place of wonder in the daytime. Each day at noon, fisherfolk gather to feed the resident fish population. As the caretaker throws morsels into the river, an astonishing spectacle unfolds. Hordes of fish, including jackfish and surgeonfish, appear seemingly out of nowhere, swarming the surface and putting on a show.

Let's go to Mayon and Cagsawa Ruins!

Breathtaking Beauty

Mayon's near-perfect symmetrical cone shape is a sight to behold, making it one of the world's most photogenic volcanoes. The lush greenery surrounding the volcano creates a stunning contrast against the blue sky.

Historical Time Capsule

The Cagsawa Ruins are a poignant reminder of the region's past, showcasing the enduring spirit of the people who lived through the eruption. The ruins are a symbol of resilience and strength.
